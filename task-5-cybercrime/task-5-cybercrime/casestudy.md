# Case Study: UPI Fraud

UPI fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes in India. It usually occurs when attackers trick users into approving fraudulent payment requests.

In a typical scenario, a scammer calls the victim pretending to be a bank representative or online seller. They convince the victim to “receive money” by approving a request in a UPI app. However, instead of receiving money, the victim actually sends money to the scammer.

Students and first-time users are common targets due to lack of awareness. The consequences include financial loss, stress, and potential misuse of personal information.

This highlights the importance of understanding how digital payment systems work and being cautious while handling unknown requests.
